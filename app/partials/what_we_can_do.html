What we can do for you

The Turn Consultancy is able to provide the following services:

  • Implementation of New Product Development (NPD) process for your products/services that are in the development stage and have not yet reached the manufacturing phase.
  • Implementation of New Product Lean Introduction (NPI) process for smooth integration for a product from development to manufacturing.
  • Business Process Analysis (BPA) for your existing product/services to identify best practices and processes in order to achieve established goals you might have regarding that product/service, such as reduced cost, fulfilling customer expectations and quality improvements.
  • Employing the Lean Six Sigma approach, a set of techniques and tools for process improvement.
  • Design for Environment (DfE) process to reduce the impact of your products on the environment, and improve social responsibility through greater efficiency and more sustainable practices.
  • Strategic Procurement and/or Supply Chain Analysis (SCA) to develop strategies that add value to your procurement activities.

We can create work-flow maps, analyse current processes and advise where we see value in changing the processes. We can develop Best Practices that are tailored to your company.

The Turn Consultancy feels that this is more effectively done by an external consultancy. It is often easier to identify waste and pinpoint where additional value can be implemented for someone who is not regularly following existing processes and can observe work flow from a fresh perspective. It is more difficult to independently analyse and recognise the need for change from within an organisation.
